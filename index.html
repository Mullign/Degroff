About DeGroff Aviation Technologies

Leading manufacturer of aviation safety equipment since 1985

DeGroff Aviation Technologies is the first and only company to design and manufacture self-disengaging pitot probe covers, revolutionizing aviation safety equipment since 1985. Our innovative PitotShield V2™ SmartCover™ (PSV2) technology prevents costly burnt-up or fouled pitot tubes in maintenance and reduces RTO (Rejected Takeoff) incidents on the runway. We serve commercial airlines, business aviation, defense contractors, and general aviation operators worldwide with products engineered for reliability, visibility, and ease of use.

Key Features of PitotShield V2™ SmartCover™ (PSV2)

  • Auto-Release Technology: SmartCover™ automatically disengages 2–5 minutes after pitot heat activation
  • Heat-Resistant Design: Engineered for auto-heated pitot probes with high-temperature materials
  • Universal Compatibility: Standard, Short, and Large options cover 90%+ of pitot probe configurations
  • Chemical Resistance: Impervious to solvents, avgas, and jet fuel exposure
  • Durable Construction: No fabric to unravel; RBF streamer options: NAS Red or High-Vis Orange with reflective accents

Technical Specifications

Body/Contact Materials Polypropylene body; only hi‑temp silicone contacts the pitot; PTFE protects the tip; no fabric to unravel
Operating/Activation Designed for auto‑heated probes; SmartCover™ auto‑releases after heat activation
Sizes Standard (V2), Short (V2‑S), Large (V2‑L) to accommodate atypical probes
Streamer Options NAS Red (24 in) or High‑Visibility Orange with reflective accent; PVC‑coated Polyester (proprietary Orange) or Nylon (NAS Red) scrim
Security Secure fit with isolation barriers; eyelets for RBF and optional lanyards
Compliance Designed with FAA/IATA safety practices in mind; shop and ramp friendly
