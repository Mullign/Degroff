About the Company

PitotShield V2 — Pitot Tube Cover

Engineered protection for pitot-static systems

Key Features

  • SmartCover™ auto-release 2–5 min after pitot heat activation
  • Heat‑resistant design for auto‑heated pitot probes
  • Universal fit: Standard, Short, and Large options cover 90%+ of probes
  • Impervious to solvents, avgas, and jet fuel
  • No fabric to unravel; RBF streamer options: NAS Red or High‑Vis Orange w/ reflective

Technical Specifications

Body/Contact Materials Polypropylene body; only hi‑temp silicone contacts the pitot; PTFE protects the tip
Operating/Activation Designed for auto‑heated probes; SmartCover™ auto‑releases after heat activation
Sizes Standard (V2), Short (V2‑S), Large (V2‑L) to accommodate atypical probes
Streamer Options NAS Red (24 in) or High‑Visibility Orange with reflective accent
Security Secure fit with isolation barriers; eyelets for RBF and optional lanyards
Compliance Designed with FAA/IATA safety practices in mind; shop and ramp friendly
